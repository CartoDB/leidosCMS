Population Density Per Hospital in the United States

This map shows the number of people per hospital facility across every state in the US. The color spectrum from light to dark reflects the increasing number of people per medical facility.



The styling on this map reflects a gradient of colors from light to dark purple, reflecting increasing population per hospital. The data distribution quintiles are defined below:
Light purple: The lowest quintile of data (0-20%)
Medium-light purple: The 2nd quintile of data (20-40%)
Medium purple: The 3rd quintile of data (40-60%)
Medium-dark purple: The 4th quintil of data (60-80%)
Dark purple: The 5th and highest quintile of data (80-100%)
